Final Fantasy is a Japanese fantasy anthology media franchise created by Hironobu Sakaguchi which is owned, developed, and published by Square Enix. The franchise centers on a series of fantasy role-playing video games.

The first game in the series was released in 1987, and since then, the series has expanded to include numerous sequels, spin-offs, and related media. The games are known for their rich storytelling, complex characters, and innovative gameplay mechanics.
